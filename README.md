This lightweight mod enhances the sleeping mechanic in Road to Vostok by letting you skip entire days while sleeping, instead of just advancing a few hours. 
Perfect for players who want to fast-forward through long waiting periods, manage hunger/thirst timers, or align their sleep schedule with better weather or safer travel conditions, or for those testing later on events.

MCM Configurable amount of days and how long the sleep screen is.
